--- title: Learning Standards Matrix date: 2018-07-24 ---

This matrix positions representative Learning Technology Standards on two dimensions. The vertical dimension is A Taxonomy of Learning Standards. The horizontal dimension is the Four-Layer Framework for Data Standards. Positioning standards according these dimensions can help education technology vendors and consumers identify the standards that are relevant to their work.

The following table is a more complete list of learning standards. Follow the links access the standards and their governing organizations. If you're aware of a standards effort that's not listed, or if a description is incomplete, please contribute your updates.

Also see the Table of Standards Organizations.
